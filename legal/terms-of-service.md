## 1. About Gennety

Gennety is an AI-first matchmaking service. Unlike traditional dating apps,
Gennety acts as the matchmaker: our AI builds a deep profile of you, proposes

Gennety launched inside university communities and keeps a first-class student
community, but it is **open to adults generally** — students sign up with a
university email and receive community perks, everyone else signs up with a
